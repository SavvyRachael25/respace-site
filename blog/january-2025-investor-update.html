reSpace Investor Update | January 2025

CNBC. 120+ new leads. The 1% Foundation. 2025 starts with a statement.

January 2025 Katrina Romatowski 5 min read

What Made January 2025 a Breakthrough Month?

If 2024 was about laying the foundation, January 2025 was the moment the world noticed. reSpace was featured on CNBC's America's Next Investment, airing three times nationally, and the response confirmed what the team already knew: the demand for co-homeownership is massive, real, and ready.

Over 120 new leads came directly from the broadcast. Not casual interest, qualified inquiries from people who saw the model and wanted in.

What Is the 1% Foundation Initiative?

Every time a reSpace suite changes ownership, 1% supports The reSpace Foundation's initiatives in financial empowerment, homelessness alleviation, and community resilience.

This isn't a marketing angle. It's a structural commitment built into the model itself. When you own with reSpace, your ownership builds more than personal wealth, it builds community infrastructure. It's what separates a company from a movement.

"We're not selling homes. We're rewriting wealth stories."

What Else Is Advancing in Q1 2025?

Presale preparation for The Leschi Collection is in its final stages. Marketing materials are production-ready. Legal documents are finalized. The broker partnership program is expanding. And The Grove's permitting continues to advance.

2025 is shaping up to be the year co-homeownership goes from concept to community. And January set the tone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How did the CNBC feature impact reSpace?

The CNBC America's Next Investment feature generated over 120 new qualified leads and significantly increased national awareness of reSpace's co-homeownership model.

What is The reSpace Foundation?

The reSpace Foundation is a 501(c)(3) that receives 1% from every property sale, funding financial empowerment, homelessness alleviation, and community resilience programs.

When will reSpace presales launch?

Presales for The Leschi Collection are targeted for Q1 2025, with final preparation underway.
