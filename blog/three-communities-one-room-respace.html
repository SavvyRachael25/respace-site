Entrepreneurial Mashup

reSpace Team · Mar 23, 2026 · 4 min read
Event Image

Three members-only communities. One night. One room. EO Seattle, GSBA, and Columbia Tower Club are converging for a private evening of founders, CEOs, and decision-makers. No outside guests. Just the people building Seattle's future.

Event Details

WhenTuesday, April 7, 2026
Time5:30 – 7:30 PM
WhereColumbia Tower Club, 75th Floor
WhoMembers only. EO, GSBA, CTC.

Who's in the Room

170+ EO members. 1,300+ GSBA businesses. Seattle's executive network. These are private, members-only organizations. Each with their own powerhouse network of founders, operators, and business leaders. On April 7th, they converge for one night only.

EO Seattle

Peer learning. No sideline advice. The local chapter of the Entrepreneurs' Organization. A global peer-to-peer network of 14,000+ founders across 60+ countries. Just entrepreneurs learning from each other.

GSBA

Equality is good business. The largest LGBTQ+ and allied chamber of commerce in North America. Championing equity in the business community since 1981. Nearly 600 scholars funded.

Columbia Tower Club

75 floors above the city. Seattle's premier private club. A community built around connection and culture, with access to 19 premier city clubs nationwide.

Why reSpace Is in This Room

reSpace was built on the belief that people thrive when they share space intentionally. Community is not an afterthought. It is the foundation. We do not just build communities inside homes. We build relationships across the ecosystem that makes Seattle one of the most forward-thinking cities in the country. The best partnerships do not happen in silos. They happen when the right people are finally in the same room. That is the power of structured connection.

Be in the room. Members of EO Seattle, GSBA, and Columbia Tower Club. April 7th. 75th floor.

Members of EO Seattle, GSBA, and Columbia Tower Club, register now to secure your spot.
