reSpace is Creating the Future of Homeownership

The housing model is broken. We're not here to fix it. We're here to replace it.

October 2024 Katrina Romatowski 6 min read

Why Does Homeownership Need a New Model?

Here's the math no one likes to talk about: the median home price in Seattle is over $850,000. The median household income can't touch it. The gap between what people earn and what homes cost hasn't been this wide since we started measuring.

The traditional path, save for a down payment, qualify for a mortgage, buy a single-family home, was built for a different economy. It works for some people. For millions of others, it's a locked door.

reSpace doesn't ask people to wait for the door to open. We built a different door.

"The housing market said no. We said watch us."

How Is reSpace Building Something New?

reSpace takes premier homes in neighborhoods people actually want to live in and uses design and technology to create small communities of individual owners. Each person owns their private suite. Each person holds a real ownership stake. Each person builds wealth.

This isn't a workaround. It's not a compromise. It's a new category, structured co-homeownership, designed from the ground up to make ownership accessible without sacrificing quality, community, or neighborhood.

The first property, The Leschi Collection, sits in one of Seattle's most sought-after neighborhoods. Views. Walkability. Character. And ownership at a price point that actually makes sense.

Who Is This For?

The locked-out professional who earns well but can't compete in a market designed for dual-income households with family money. The remote worker who wants a Seattle base without a Seattle mortgage. The retiree who wants community, not isolation. The parent helping their adult child get a foothold.

reSpace is for anyone who's been told that ownership isn't for them, and refuses to believe it.

The movement is real: 70% of Gen Z are open to co-buying a home. reSpace is building the infrastructure to make that possible, legally sound, financially structured, and community-centered.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is reSpace?

reSpace is a structured co-homeownership company that transforms single-family homes in premier Seattle neighborhoods into small communities of individual owners, each holding a membership interest in a single-purpose LLC.

How is reSpace different from traditional homeownership?

Traditional homeownership requires buying an entire property. reSpace lets you own a private suite in a shared luxury home, with all the legal protections, wealth-building potential, and independence of traditional ownership at a fraction of the cost.

Is reSpace available outside of Seattle?

reSpace is currently focused on Seattle's premier neighborhoods. Expansion plans are underway as the model is proven and scaled.

How do I learn more about co-homeownership with reSpace?

Visit respace.co to explore the model, learn about The Leschi Collection, and see if structured co-homeownership is right for you.
