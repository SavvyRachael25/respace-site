reSpace Test Market Takes Over Seattle NBC News: Featured as King5's Top Story for 24-Hour News Cycle

When Seattle's biggest news station leads with your story for an entire day, the city is paying attention.

July 2024 Katrina Romatowski 5 min read
As Featured In King5 NBC Seattle

Why Did King5 Make reSpace the Top Story?

When King5 NBC — Seattle's most-watched news station — decides to lead with a story for an entire 24-hour news cycle, it's not because they needed filler. It's because the story hit a nerve.

reSpace's test market in Seattle did exactly that. The segment explored how structured co-homeownership is creating a new path to ownership for people priced out of the traditional market — and the response from viewers was immediate and sustained.

The coverage wasn't a puff piece. It walked through the model. It talked to real people. It asked the hard questions. And the conclusion was clear: this is something Seattle needs.

What Did the King5 Coverage Reveal?

The segment highlighted a truth that Seattle already knew but hadn't seen a solution for: a massive gap between what people earn and what homes cost. And into that gap, reSpace placed something nobody expected — a real, structured, legally sound model that makes ownership possible at a fraction of the traditional cost.

Viewers saw the property. They heard from the founder. They understood the model. And then they did what people do when something finally makes sense — they shared it.

"This isn't a workaround. This isn't a compromise. This is the new way to own." — Katrina Romatowski on King5

What Does This Media Attention Mean for Co-Homeownership?

Major media coverage validates what reSpace has been building. When the region's most trusted news source puts co-homeownership in front of hundreds of thousands of viewers, it moves the conversation from "is this real?" to "how do I get involved?"

The King5 feature was a signal: co-homeownership isn't an experiment anymore. It's a movement. And Seattle is watching.

Frequently Asked Questions

Was reSpace really King5's top story for a full day?

Yes. reSpace's test market was featured as King5 NBC Seattle's top story for a complete 24-hour news cycle, appearing across morning, afternoon, and evening broadcasts.

What is reSpace's test market?

reSpace's test market in Seattle is the first structured co-homeownership property — The Leschi Collection — designed to prove the model in one of the city's most desirable neighborhoods.

What is co-homeownership?

Co-homeownership is a structured ownership model where buyers own a membership interest in a single-purpose LLC, with exclusive-use rights to a private suite and shared-use rights to common areas.

Visit respace.co to explore the model, see the properties, and understand how structured co-homeownership works.
