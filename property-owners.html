What we do

We transform homes into communities of owners.

reSpace uses technology and design to transform single-family homes in premier neighborhoods into small communities of individual owners. Each co-owner holds a membership interest in a single-purpose LLC with exclusive-use rights to a private suite and shared-use rights to common areas.

Your property becomes something more valuable than the market is telling you. We can show you what that looks like.

The model

How it works for property owners.

  • We evaluate your property and show you what it could become as a co-homeownership community
  • Your property remains a single residential asset owned by a single-purpose LLC
  • Each co-homeowner purchases a membership interest with exclusive-use rights to a designated suite
  • Homes are designed for owner-occupancy, not investors or short-term rentals
  • reSpace handles the renovation, legal structure, financing, and co-owner matching
  • Co-homeowners can sell their ownership interest with right-of-first-refusal for existing co-owners
  • Easy-out option: the LLC may repurchase an ownership interest for the original purchase price
  • In-house financing available for buyers, with approvals in 7 to 10 days
  • 3% buyer broker commission on every closed transaction, paid through escrow
  • 1% of every property sale goes to The reSpace Foundation, a 501(c)(3)

Questions

Clear answers. No pitch.

What happens to my property?

+

reSpace transforms your single-family home into a structured co-homeownership community. The property is owned by a single-purpose LLC. Each co-homeowner purchases a membership interest with exclusive-use rights to a private suite and shared-use rights to common areas. The property remains a single residential asset.

Do I have to sell my property outright?

+

Not necessarily. We can discuss several options depending on your goals. The free analysis will show you what your property could look like as a co-homeownership community and what your options are. No obligation to proceed with anything.

Who ends up living in my home?

+

Owner-occupants only. reSpace properties are designed for people who live in them. No investors, no short-term rentals. Co-homeowners are matched based on lifestyle, goals, and timeline. The first group of co-homeowners defines the house rules together.

How is this different from selling to a developer?

+

Developers demolish and rebuild. reSpace preserves and transforms. Your home becomes a community of owners in the neighborhood you helped build. The value is unlocked, not destroyed. And 1% of every property sale goes to The reSpace Foundation.

What if I decide to do nothing?

+

That is a perfectly valid outcome. The analysis is free. It shows you what is possible. If the answer is "not right now," you still have the information. No pressure, no follow-up unless you want it.
