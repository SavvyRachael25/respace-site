The New Way to Own

What Is Co-Homeownership?

reSpace transforms single-family homes in premier neighborhoods into small communities of individual owners. You get a private suite and shared living spaces for what you already pay to rent.

Your Own Private Suite

Ensuite bath, walk-in closet, private washer/dryer, wet bar, sitting area, and workspace. Your space. Your lock.

Shared Living Spaces

Designer kitchen, co-working space, media lounge, and landscaped backyard. Community as an asset.

Real Ownership, Not Rent

Membership interest in a single-purpose LLC. Your own loan. Not tied to other co-owners.

All-Inclusive Monthly

Home loan, taxes, insurance, maintenance, landscaping, housekeeping, and property management.

Premier Neighborhood

West Leschi. Traditional homes start at $1.5M+. With reSpace, own starting at $124,500.

Owner-Occupied Only

Every co-owner lives here. No short-term rentals. A real community of people who chose this home.
