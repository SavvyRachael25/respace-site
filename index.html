The process

Seven steps to owning your home.

01
Find your property
Browse our current properties in the neighborhoods you actually want to live in. See something you like? That is where this starts.
02
Apply
Submit your application. We review your financials, your goals, and your timeline. No W-2 required.
03
Get financed
Our in-house financing team works with you directly. Approval in 7 to 10 days. Built for gig workers, self-employed buyers, and anyone conventional lenders have turned away.
04
Form your group
Bring your people or we help you find them. Each co-owner reserves a membership interest with exclusive-use rights to their suite.
05
Close
We handle the legal structure. The LLC, the operating agreement, the terms. You sign. You get the keys.
06
Move in
Your suite is yours. The common spaces are shared. The neighborhood is home. You are no longer renting.
07
Own your future
Full appreciation retained as the property grows in value. Your membership interest can be sold with right-of-first-refusal for your co-owners. This is ownership, not a lease with extra steps.
